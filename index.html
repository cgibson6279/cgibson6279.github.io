Graduate Center, The City College of New York

I am a PhD candidate in linguistics at the Graduate Center, City University of New York. I also work as a data engineer embedded in Google via TekSystems. Some research topics of interests include NLP for low-resource languages, BookNLP, and conversational AI. In my spare time I enjoy gaming, reading, watching esports and traditional sports, and playing Magic the Gathering with friends.
